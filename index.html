Preface Purpose

Root Logos is not a blog, publication, or digital notebook. It is a living, corrigible language for composing coherent participation.

The objective is not to accumulate ideas. The objective is to discover the primitive relations from which coherent perception, conduct, creation, and institutions can be generated. Pages preserve the path; the grammar must eventually exceed its scaffolding.
