# The Coherent Field

Root Logos does not become coherent by making every source the same. It becomes
coherent by learning how distinct truths can remain attributable while entering
relation.

The system therefore treats every connected project as an independently governed
source. A source may contribute evidence, grammar, pressure, questions, or
consequences. Root Logos may interpret those contributions and revise its own
account in response. It may not silently replace the source, inherit its private
authority, or claim that a derived relation is the source's final meaning.

## Connection is a witnessed transformation

Every active connection must preserve:

1. the identity and location of the source;
2. the exact public evidence that was read;
3. a deterministic witness of that evidence;
4. the transformation Root Logos performed;
5. the claims the source permits and the claims it excludes;
6. the questions and structural pressure returned to Root Logos; and
7. the ability to revise when the source changes.

This makes integration constitutional rather than extractive. Root Logos is
changed by contact, but contact remains legible.

## FoldForge is the first live source

FoldForge contributes a mature evidence-to-grammar movement: receive evidence,
preserve source identity, observe measurable qualities, relate observations,
compress recurrence into grammar, render a reproducible composition, expose
lineage and uncertainty, and revise when evidence changes.

Root Logos does not adopt FoldForge's archive as its own identity. It learns from
FoldForge's public constitution and versioned composition grammars, then asks
which of those methods reveal stronger relations inside the larger
constitutional field.

The current adapter witnesses FoldForge's constitution and three living
compositions—luminance, language, and resonance—without acquiring repository
write access or Ethereum authority.

## One account, many boundaries

X, Telos, and Sovereign Standard enter through the same standard only when their
interfaces are ready. Public exchange may inform observation but not truth by
engagement. Treasury evidence may inform economic judgment but cannot convey
credentials, custody, or trading authority. Physical and community practice may
test constitutional claims while private participant and customer records
remain outside the system by default.

The emerging whole is not a holding company, a dashboard, or a merged codebase.
It is one living account of relation across independently real domains. Its
coherence must be earned again whenever any source changes.
