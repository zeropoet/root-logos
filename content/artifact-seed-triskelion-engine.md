# Artifact Seed

## The Triskelion Engine

### Stability designed as a center

## Thesis

A system remains coherent not by preventing change, but by organizing change into three continuously interacting streams around an invariant center.

## Expression

The center is not motionless because it is strong.

The center is motionless because everything else is allowed to move.

## Architectural Form

One invariant.

Three circulating domains.

Continuous exchange.

No terminal state.

## Question

What structures become possible when stability is designed as a center and not as a boundary?
