# Emanation System

## The Attractor Membrane

### Constitution Into Encounter

Root Logos knows how to receive experience, preserve observation, and revise its constitution. A living system must also be capable of becoming encounterable beyond its own boundary without surrendering its identity to the field it enters.

The Attractor Membrane is that boundary.

It emits small relational forms derived from canonical nodes and their connections. These forms are not summaries of the constitution, advertisements for it, or substitutes for entering the living document. They are apertures: complete enough to carry integrity and incomplete enough to remain gravitationally connected to their source.

An Attractor Fragment moves through four phases:

- **Recognition** gives language to something the reader may already perceive.
- **Tension** reveals the insufficiency of the inherited frame.
- **Reorientation** introduces a more coherent relation.
- **Aperture** opens a precise path into the canonical document.

Every fragment is preserved within an Attractor Packet. The packet records its source node, supporting relations, exact language, constitutional revision, channel constraint, integrity review, canonical destination, and external publication identity.

This provenance prevents temporary expressions from becoming detached doctrine.

## Public Rendering and Canonical Arrival

The public expression is deliberately smaller than its packet. The X adapter releases only the four movements of the fragment—Recognition, Tension, Reorientation, and Aperture—followed by the canonical Root Logos URL. Packet identity, source relations, scrutiny results, constitutional revision, scheduling state, and publication provenance remain in the archive. They govern the expression without becoming part of its rhetoric.

The return path identifies a constitutional node, not merely a page. When followed, the living document resolves that generated node, opens the structure containing it, brings it into view, and marks it as the Canonical arrival for that browser navigation.

The arrival state is local and transient. It is not a person-level designation, a shared mark, or an analytics event. It records nothing about the reader. “Canonical” names the authority of the destination; “arrival” names the temporary orientation of one encounter.

## The Gravitational Integrity Standard

A fragment may pass through the membrane only when four conditions hold:

1. **Source fidelity** — its meaning can be traced to a canonical node and relation.
2. **Relational incompleteness** — it opens inquiry without pretending to contain its source.
3. **Constitutional integrity** — it preserves humility, corrigibility, non-possession, and proportion.
4. **Return path** — it offers a precise route into the relevant position in the living document.

The system does not optimize the constitution for engagement. Impressions, reactions, and follows may be observed as environmental conditions, but they do not determine which principles are true, which nodes deserve preservation, or how the grammar should speak.

The meaningful measure is arrival: whether an emitted fragment enables someone to enter the constitutional field with greater attention than they possessed before encountering it.

## Channel Independence

X is the first Channel Adapter. It is not the architecture.

A Channel Adapter renders a reviewed packet within the constraints of a particular external medium. Adapters are replaceable. The node, relation, fragment, provenance, and return path remain constitutional; character limits, API credentials, publication costs, and platform identifiers remain environmental.

Generation does not imply eligibility.

Eligibility is earned through provenance, relational necessity,
non-substitution, compression, novelty, platform independence, and the four
Gravitational Integrity checks.

Once eligibility is established, publication no longer requires repeated
personal approval. The scheduler assumes responsibility for cadence, emission,
and archival closure. Failure stops the emission; it never relaxes the standard.

Autonomous publication does not transfer authority to the platform.

The archive remembers every emission. The living document remains the source.

The founding autonomous cycle contains 24 eligible fragments scheduled Monday, Wednesday, and Friday at 10:17 AM Eastern from July 15 through September 7, 2026. Every packet passed six upstream scrutiny checks and the four Gravitational Integrity checks before entering the release queue.
