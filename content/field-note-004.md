# Field Note 004

## Architecture Beyond Euphoria

### Structures capable of carrying intention

The desire to build usually exceeds the ability to sustain the emotional intensity of beginning. Euphoria is not the engine of creation but its ignition. What endures is architecture: structures capable of carrying intention long after inspiration has receded. The mature creator no longer depends on extraordinary motivation. They construct systems that continue the work until joy returns of its own accord.
