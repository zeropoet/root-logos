# Field Note

## Behind the Canvas

### Creation as remembering

The only war is behind a canvas.

It is the place where possibility resists becoming form, where every mark closes one world to reveal another.

And somehow, it is also the only place I consistently recover what life keeps teaching me to forget: that creation is less an act of invention than an act of remembering.
